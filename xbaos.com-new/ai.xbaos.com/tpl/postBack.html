Tracking URL’s

You can now add up to 5 dynamic parameters, 512 characters each. The dynamic-parameter can be reported back to you on external pixels and callbacks by the corresponding parameter name (clickid, gaid, idfa etc.)

i.e :

https://m.z2z.org/t?appid=12345&affid={{currentId}}&pubid={{publisherid}}&gaid={{gaid}}&idfa={{idfa}}&srcid={{scrid}}&refid={{clickid}}

https://m.z2z.org/t?appid=10002&affid=1024&pubid={publisher}&gaid={gaid}&aaid={aaid}&idfa={idfa}&idfv={idfv}&srcid={traffic}&refid={clickid}

Declaration :

Parameter pubid is digital account of affiliate (default 1, nil or nan default 0), used for monitoring quality, optimizing quality and controlling the risk.
Our system is designed for matching the target ads precisely, means the more complete of para, the more accuracy of targeting. Or it will target to other AD position and maintain the price.
Needn’t to worry about the waste of traffic even if running out the budget, our system will switch the mode of targeting ads precisely to the mode of cash the flow automatically. Will send notification mail simultaneously.
Paused campaign will switch into long Tail Business Model automatically if we can’t remove it immediately. All modes go with machine learning and bidding to guarantee the earnings. Will send notification mail simultaneously.
Because of the difference of conversion model, please upload equipment parameters, like, GAID and AAID for Android platform, IDFA and IDFV for IOS platform.
